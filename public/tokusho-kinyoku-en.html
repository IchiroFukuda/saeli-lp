Returns and Cancellation

1. Returns and Refunds

Due to the nature of digital content, we do not accept returns or refunds after purchase confirmation.

2. Cancellation (Stopping Auto-Renewal)

  • If you do not cancel at least 24 hours before the end of your subscription period, your subscription will automatically renew.
  • Deleting the app or logging out does not complete the cancellation process. Please proceed through your device settings (Apple ID or Google Play subscription management screen).
  • If you cancel during the subscription period, you will continue to have access to premium features until the end of that period, and no pro-rated refunds will be issued.

Detailed Cancellation Instructions

For iOS (iPhone / iPad)

  1. Open the "Settings" app
  2. Tap your Apple ID at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select "Kinyoku Monkey"
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

For Android

  1. Open the Google Play Store app
  2. Tap your profile icon in the upper right
  3. Tap "Payments & subscriptions" → "Subscriptions"
  4. Select "Kinyoku Monkey"
  5. Tap "Cancel subscription"
