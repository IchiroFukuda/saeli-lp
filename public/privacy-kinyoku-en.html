Privacy Policy

SAELI LLC (hereinafter referred to as "the Company") establishes this Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as "this Policy") regarding the handling of users' personal information in the Kinyoku Monkey app (hereinafter referred to as "the App").

1. About the App

Kinyoku Monkey is a comprehensive application that supports users in achieving their goals through habit tracking and management, abstinence period measurement, and motivation support.

2. Information We Collect

The App collects only the minimum necessary information for service provision.

  • Automatically Collected Information: Device identifiers (Device ID), OS version, app version, usage logs, crash reports, etc.
  • Payment Information: Purchase tokens and subscription status provided by payment processors during in-app purchases/subscriptions (we do not store credit card information)
  • Inquiry Information: Email address, content, etc., provided when you contact us

※Important: The App does not collect any direct personal information such as name, address, or phone number. Additionally, in-app data such as habit records and counters are stored only on the user's device and are not transmitted to our servers.

3. Purpose of Use

  • To provide, guarantee operation, and improve the quality of the App
  • For payment processing and subscription management
  • To detect and fix crashes and bugs
  • For statistical analysis of usage patterns (in a format that does not identify individuals) to improve functionality
  • For inquiry response, identity verification, and fraud prevention
  • To respond to legal requirements or requests from public institutions

4. Use of External Services

The App uses the following external services, which may automatically collect and process certain information. Please refer to each provider's privacy policy for details.

4.1 Firebase (Google LLC)

  • Purpose: Usage analysis, crash report collection
  • Information Collected: Device information, usage logs, crash information, etc.
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 RevenueCat

  • Purpose: In-app purchase and subscription management
  • Information Collected: Purchase tokens, subscription status, device identifiers, etc.
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

4.3 Apple / Google Payment Services

  • Purpose: Payment processing for in-app purchases
  • Information Collected: Purchase history, payment information, etc. (managed by each platform provider)
  • Privacy Policy:
    • Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
    • Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Third-Party Provision of Personal Information

The Company will not provide personal information to third parties without your consent, except as permitted by law or through necessary information sharing with the external services mentioned above.

6. Security Management of Information

The Company implements necessary and appropriate measures to prevent leakage, loss, or damage to personal information. We also select external service providers that implement appropriate security measures.

7. Data Retention Period

  • In-App Data: Stored on the device until the user uninstalls the App
  • Analytics Data: Retained as anonymized statistical information for the period necessary for service improvement
  • Purchase Information: Retained by RevenueCat and platform providers for the period necessary for payment and subscription management

8. User Rights

You may make the following requests regarding personal data held by the Company:

  • Request for disclosure
  • Request for correction, addition, or deletion
  • Request for suspension of use

If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at the inquiry contact below. We will respond appropriately in accordance with the law after verifying your identity.

9. Minor Users

To Parents/Guardians: The App does not have age restrictions, but if minors use it, please supervise their use. Minors should not provide personal information without parental consent.

10. Changes to This Policy

The Company may change this Policy in accordance with legal changes or app feature improvements. If there are significant changes, we will notify you through in-app notifications or on our website.

11. Governing Law and Jurisdiction

This Policy shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Japan, and the Tokyo District Court shall have exclusive jurisdiction as the court of first instance for any disputes concerning this Policy.
