Privacy Policy

SAELI LLC ("we," "us," or "the Company") establishes the following Privacy Policy ("this Policy") regarding the handling of personal information of users in connection with our websites, mobile applications, and other services ("the Services").

1. Information We Collect

  • Information you provide when contacting us (name, email address, message content, etc.)
  • Information automatically transmitted through app usage (device information, identifiers, logs, cookies, etc.)
  • Information provided by payment processors when using in-app purchases or subscriptions (purchase tokens, subscription status, etc.)

2. Purpose of Use

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Services
  • To respond to inquiries, verify identity, and prevent unauthorized use
  • To measure and analyze usage, and improve quality
  • To comply with legal requirements or requests from public authorities

3. Third-Party Services and Data Collection

We may use the following third-party services for quality improvement, payment processing, and other purposes. These service providers may collect certain information for usage measurement and payment processing. Please refer to each provider's privacy policy for details.

  • Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK) — For ad delivery and measurement. Advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID) may be used. See Google Privacy Policy for details.
  • Google (Google Play / Firebase, etc.)

  • Other services necessary for analytics, distribution, and payment processing

3-1. Use of Advertising Identifiers (iOS)

Our iOS apps may request permission to access the advertising identifier (IDFA) through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework for the purpose of delivering relevant advertisements. You can still use the app's core features without granting permission, but ads displayed may not be personalized.

4. Disclosure of Personal Information to Third Parties

We will not provide personal information to third parties without the user's consent, except as permitted by law.

5. Security

We take necessary and appropriate measures to prevent leakage, loss, or damage of personal information and to ensure its security.

6. Disclosure, Correction, and Suspension of Use

If you wish to request disclosure, correction, addition, deletion, or suspension of use of your personal data held by us, please contact us at hello@saeli.org. After verifying your identity, we will respond appropriately in accordance with applicable laws.

7. Minors

We ask that minors do not provide personal information without the consent of a parent or guardian. We may require parental consent verification when necessary.

8. Compliance and Revisions
